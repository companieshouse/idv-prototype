Use this service to register your business as an authorised agent with Companies House.

Who needs to register as an authorised agent?

From XX DATE, individuals or organisations will need to register as a Companies House authorised agent to (do any of the following):

  • file on behalf of clients
  • form companies or registrable partnerships on behalf of clients
  • verify someone's identity for Companies House

To apply, you must be (one of the following):

  • director
  • general partner
  • partner
  • sole trader

Before you start

If you’re a sole trader, you’ll need to verify your identity for Companies House before you can use this service.

Verify your identity for Companies House.


What you'll need

You’ll need:

  • to give details about your business, or yourself if you're a sole trader
  • the names of all Anti-Money Laundering (AML) supervisory bodies the business is registered with
  • the AML membership numbers - we will check this with your AML supervisory body
  • to pay the £XX registration fee using a credit or debit card
We'll check the AML supervisory body has the same name and membership number for your business. If you're registered with the AML supervisory body as an individual, you'll need to apply as a sole trader using your own name.

This service will time out if you do not use it for 60 minutes. You can save your answers and resume your application later.
