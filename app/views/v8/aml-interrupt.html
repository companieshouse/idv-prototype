Your business must be registered with an AML supervisory body

To confirm your Anti-Money Laundering (AML) registration, we need to check the name of your business is the same as the one your supervisory body holds.

If the AML registration is in your name, the details will not match and we will reject your application.

What to do next

To register your business as an authorised agent, you'll need to register the business with an AML supervisory body first.

Or, to register yourself as an authorised agent, you can apply as a sole trader.
