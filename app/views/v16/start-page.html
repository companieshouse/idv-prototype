Use this service to register yourself, or your business as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP). ACSPs are also known as authorised agents.

For now, you only need to register to verify people's identities for Companies House.

Who can apply

To register your business, you must be one of the following:

  • director
  • general partner
  • partner
  • member
  • sole trader
If you’re registering an unincorporated body or corporate body

Corporate body

If the body is managed by its members, you must be a member of the body. Otherwise, you must be the equivalent of a director.

Unincorporated entity

If the body is managed by its members, you must be a member of the body. Otherwise, you must be a member of the governing body.

You’ll be able to add other people who work for the business to the authorised agent account once you’ve registered.

Before you start

You'll need to verify your identity

If you haven't already verified your identity for Companies House, we'll take you to the service to do this before you apply. To find out what you'll need, read our guidance on how to verify your identity for Companies House.

Check your Anti-Money Laundering (AML) details

The business must be supervised by a UK Anti-Money Laundering (AML) supervisory body. To confirm this, you'll need to tell us:

  • which AML supervisory bodies the business is registered with
  • the AML membership numbers (sometimes known as your practice number or firm ID)

If we cannot confirm the supervisory body has the same details for your business, we’ll reject your application.

Contact your AML supervisory body if you need to check your details.

What you'll need

The details you need to provide will depend on the type of business you're registering. You should read the guidance on applying to register as an authorised agent to check you have all the details you'll need to provide.

You’ll need to:

  • sign in to or create a GOV.UK One Login to use this service
  • pay the £55 registration fee

You can save your answers and resume your application later if you need to.
