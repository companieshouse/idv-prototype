Update an authorised agents details

Use this service to tell us about a person whose identity you have verified.

You must be registered as a Companies House authorised agent to use this service.

The checks you do to verify someone’s identity must meet Companies House identity verification standard. Read the guidance to find out how you can meet the standard.

What you'll need

You'll need to enter the person's details as they are shown on the documents you used to check their identity.

You’ll need to tell us their:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • home address

You should also provide an email address, if they have one.

It takes around 10 minutes to use this service.
