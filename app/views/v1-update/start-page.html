Update an authorised agent's details

Use this service to tell us about an update to your authorised agent details.

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "You must tell us about any changes to the agent's Anti-Money Laundering (AML) registration within 14 days.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

What you'll need

You'll need to tell us which details have changed, then give the updated details.

We'll usually email you within 2 working days to let you know if your update has been accepted or rejected.

If you're updating details that we show on the public list of authorised agents, we'll show the new details if your update is accepted.

Details we'll show on the public list of authorised agents

We will update the information shown on the public list of authorised agents if you change the:

  • name of your business, or you own name if you’re a sole trader and do not have a different business name
  • town or city of your business address, or your correspondence address if you’re a sole trader
  • AML supervisory bodies your business is registered with

It takes around 5 minutes to use this service.
