Use this service to register your business as an authorised agent with Companies House. Authorised agents are also known as Authorised Corporate Service Providers (ACSPs).

You'll need to register your business as an authorised agent to be able to verify your clients' identities for Companies House.

From XX DATE, businesses will also need to register to be able to file on behalf of clients.

Who can apply

To register your business as an authorised agent, you must be one of the following:

  • director
  • general partner
  • partner
  • member
  • sole trader
If you’re registering an unincorporated body or corporate body

Corporate body

If the body is managed by its members, you must be a member of the body. Otherwise, you must be the equivalent of a director.

Unincorporated entity

If the body is managed by its members, you must be a member of the body. Otherwise, you must be a member of the governing body.

You’ll be able to add other people who work for the business to the authorised agent account once you’ve registered.

How to apply

1. Sign in and verify your identity

You'll need to sign in to, or create a GOV.UK One Login to use this service. The email address you use will be the one you use to access the authorised agent account once you've registered.

We'll ask you to verify your identity when you sign in, if you have not already done this. To check what you'll need, read the guidance about verifying your identity for Companies House.

2. Tell us information about the business, or yourself if you're a sole trader

The information you need to provide will depend on the type of business you're registering. To check you have everything you need, read the guidance on applying to register as an authorised agent.

3. Provide Anti-Money Laundering (AML) registration details

You’ll need to tell us which AML supervisory bodies the business is registered with and provide the AML membership numbers or IDs. Contact your AML supervisory body if you need to check your details.

4. Pay the £55 registration fee

You'll need to pay using a credit or debit card.

You can save your answers and resume your application later if you need to.
