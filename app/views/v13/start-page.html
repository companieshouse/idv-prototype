Use this service to register your business as an authorised agent with Companies House. Authorised agents are also known as Authorised Corporate Service Providers (ACSPs).

Who needs to register as an authorised agent?

From XX DATE, individuals or organisations will need to register as a Companies House authorised agent to (do any of the following):

  • file on behalf of clients
  • form companies or registrable partnerships on behalf of clients
  • verify someone's identity for Companies House

To apply, the business must already be registered with a UK Anti-Money Laundering (AML) supervisory body. For example, accountants, solicitors or company formation agents.

To apply, you must be one of the following for the business you are registering:

  • director
  • general partner
  • member
  • sole trader
You'll be able to add other people who work for the business once you have registered.

Before you start

You’ll need to verify your identity for Companies House before you can use this service.

Verify your identity for Companies House.


What you'll need

You’ll need:

  • to give details about your business, or yourself if you're a sole trader
  • the names of all Anti-Money Laundering (AML) supervisory bodies the business is registered with - you must have been registered for at least 2 weeks
  • the AML membership numbers or IDs 
  • to pay the £XX registration fee using a credit or debit card
We'll confirm the AML supervisory body has the same name and membership number for your business. Contact your AML supervisory body if you need to check your details before you start.

This service will time out if you do not use it for 60 minutes. You can save your answers and resume your application later.
