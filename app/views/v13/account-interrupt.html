We have changed the way you sign in to some of our services

We’ll ask you to sign in or create a GOV.UK One Login, which will allow you to access many government services using the same email address and password.

You can save your existing information

If you use the same email address for your GOV.UK One Login as your existing Companies House account, we'll save any information stored, such as:

  • your filing history
  • companies you follow
What is a Companies House account?

This is the account you can use to sign in to:

  • the Companies House register - sometimes known as 'Companies House service' or 'Find and update company information'
  • some of our online services - for example, 'Register your company' or 'Upload a document to Companies House'

A Companies House account is not the same as WebFiling.
