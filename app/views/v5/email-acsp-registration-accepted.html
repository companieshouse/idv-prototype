We have approved your application and registered MORRIS ACCOUNTING LTD as a Companies House authorised agent. This is also known as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP).

The authorised agent number for MORRIS ACCOUNTING LTD is: 06254821

Keep the number secure and do not share it with anyone outside of your organisation. You will need this for paper filings.

What happens next

When you sign in to your Companies House account, you’ll be able to access services for authorised agents.

Add or remove members who work for the authorised agent

Once added, members can sign in to their account to:

  • form companies and registrable partnerships on behalf of clients
  • file on behalf of clients
  • verify someone's identity

Update the authorised agent’s details

You must provide details about any changes to the agent's Anti-Money Laundering (AML) registration.

Tell us you have verified someone’s identity

You do not have to offer identity verification to your clients.

If you choose to verify someone’s identity for the Companies House register, you must keep a copy of any documents you use to check the person’s identity.

You’ll need to keep these while you have a working relationship and for 5 years after.
