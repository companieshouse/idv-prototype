Use this service to register as an authorised agent with Companies House.

To register an authorised agent, you must be one of the following:

  • director
  • general partner
  • partner
  • sole trader

What you'll need

You will need to give details about the business, including the:

  • UK registered address, or correspondence address if you're a sole trader
  • names of all AML supervisory bodies the business is registered with
  • AML membership numbers

You'll need to pay the £XX registration fee using a credit or debit card.

Identity verification

If you’re a sole trader, you’ll need to verify your identity for Companies House before you can use this service.
