Use this service to register your business as an authorised agent with Companies House.

To apply, you must be one of the following:

  • director
  • general partner
  • partner
  • sole trader

What you'll need

You’ll need to give details about your business, or yourself if you are a sole trader. You'll also need the:

  • names of all AML supervisory bodies the business is registered with
  • AML membership numbers
We will check the AML supervisory body has the same name and membership number for your business. If you are registered personally, you will need to apply as a sole trader.

You'll need to pay the £XX registration fee using a credit or debit card.

Verify your identity

If you’re a sole trader, you’ll need to verify your identity for Companies House before you can use this service.
