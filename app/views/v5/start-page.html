Use this service to register as an authorised agent with Companies House.

To register an authorised agent, you must be one of the following:

  • director
  • general partner
  • partner
  • sole trader

What you'll need

You will need to give details about the business, including the:

  • UK registered address
  • names of all AML supervisory bodies the business is registered with
  • AML membership numbers

You'll also need to pay the £XX registration fee using a credit or debit card.

If you're a sole trader you'll also need to prove your identity with a GOV.UK One Login, if you have not done this already.

What you’ll need to verify your identity

You'll need:

  • to sign in or create a GOV.UK One Login
  • a biometric passport, or a UK photocard driving licence
  • your current home address (you might also need your previous address if you’ve recently moved)
