Your company must be registered with an AML supervisory body

To confirm your anti-money laundering (AML) registration, we need to check that the name of your company is the same as the one your supervisory body holds.

If the AML registration is in your own name, the details will not match. We will need to reject your application.

You can either:

  • register your company with an AML supervisory body, then apply to register as an authorised agent, or
  • apply to register yourself as an authorised agent – you will need to select ‘sole trader’ as the type of business
