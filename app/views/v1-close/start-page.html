Close a Companies House authorised agent

Use this service to tell us if MORRIS ACCOUNTING LTD no longer needs to be a Companies House authorised agent. This is also known as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP).

What happens when you close an authorised agent

We will close the online authorised agent account. You and any other users that have been added to the account will not be able to:

  • file on behalf of clients
  • verify people’s identity for Companies House

We’ll also publish a notice to show the agent has stopped being an authorised agent. This will show the:

  • name of the business
  • date the status changed
