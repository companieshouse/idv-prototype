Close a Companies House authorised agent

Use this service to tell us if MORRIS ACCOUNTING LTD no longer needs to be a Companies House authorised agent. This is also known as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP).

What you'll need

You'll need to confirm a declaration to show you understand what happens when you have closed the authorised agent.

Before you continue, you should:

  • check if you need agreement from anyone else in the business - this could someone who has the role of a director or equivalent
  • let any other users who have been added to the account know they will no longer be able to access the authorised agent account or services
