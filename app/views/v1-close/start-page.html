Closing an Authorised corporate service provider (ACSP)

Use this service to register your business as an authorised agent with Companies House. Authorised agents are also known as Authorised Corporate Service Providers (ACSPs).

To apply, you must be one of the following for the business you are registering:

  • director
  • general partner
  • member
  • sole trader

What you'll need

You’ll need:

  • to give details about your business, or yourself if you're a sole trader
  • the names of all Anti-Money Laundering (AML) supervisory bodies the business is registered with - you must have been registered for at least 2 weeks
  • the AML membership numbers or IDs 
  • to pay the £XX registration fee using a credit or debit card
