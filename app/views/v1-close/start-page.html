Close the Companies House authorised agent

If you close the authorised agent, MORRIS ACCOUNTING LTD will no longer be registered as an authorised agent with Companies House. This is also known as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP).

Before you close the authorised agent, you must check if there are other users who have been added to account. You'll need to tell them they will not be able to access the account, or use services for the authorised agent.
