Your obligations as an ACSP

  • If you cease to be AML supervised, you must notify the registrar and provide information relating to the circumstances.
  • If you change AML supervision, and are now supervised by another organisation, you MUST inform us of the new details.
  • If you change roles and no longer wish to be a point of contact for this ACSP, you must notify the registrar of the replacement officer details.
