What you'll need

To register a company or individual as an ACSP, you’ll need:

  • to verify your own identity with a GOV.UK account, if you have not done this already
  • the name of the company, or your own name if you are self-employed
  • the registered address
  • the name of the Anti-Money Laundering (AML) supervisory body the business is registered with
  • the AML registration number
  • to pay the £XX registration fee using a credit or debit card
