To register as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP), you need to first prove your identity.

The registration needs to be made by the Money Laundering Reporting Officer (MLRO), a director or partner.

You need to be registered and AML supervised in the UK.

Before you start

To prove your identity, you'll need:

  • a GOV.UK account – we’ll ask you to create or sign in to your account
  • your photo ID
  • your current home address (you might also need your previous address if you’ve recently moved)

To register as an ACSP, you'll need:

  • individual or company name
  • registered office address
  • legal form of the ACSP
  • supervisory body of the ACSP

Registering takes around 10 minutes.
