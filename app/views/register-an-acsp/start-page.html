Use this service to register an ACSP with Companies House.

You need to register as an ACSP to:

  • verify the identity of officers for the Companies House register
  • form companies or registrable partnerships, or to file on their behalf

What you'll need

To register a company or individual as an ACSP, you’ll need:

  • to verify your own identity with a GOV.UK account, if you have not done this already
  • the name of the company, or your own name if you are self-employed
  • the registered address
  • the name of the Anti-Money Laundering (AML) supervisory body the business is registered with
  • the AML registration number
  • to pay the £XX registration fee using a credit or debit card

You must be the person who is responsible for Anti-Money Laundering (AML) compliance within the business to apply to register the ACSP.

You’ll need to sign in to your Companies House account to start using this service.
