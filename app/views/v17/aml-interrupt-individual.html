*You must be registered with an AML supervisory body under your name *

***If you are registering as a sole practitioner, you need to be AML supervised under your name.***

To confirm your Anti-Money Laundering (AML) registration, we need to check the **your name** is the same as the one your supervisory body holds.

If the AML registration is in a different name, the details will not match and we will reject your application.

Once you have confirmed you are AML supervised under your name, you can continue with your application.

Company (firm) is registered for AML

If your company is regisered for AML, you will need to register, not as a sole practitioner, but as a company (firm).

Register your company as an ACSP

What to do next

To register your company as an authorised agent, you'll need to register the company name with an AML supervisory body first.

Or, to register yourself as an authorised agent, you can apply as a sole trader.
