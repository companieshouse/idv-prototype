We have approved your application and registered MORRIS ACCOUNTING LTD (02050399) as a Companies House authorised agent. This is also known as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP).

Keep the number secure and do not share it with anyone outside of your organisation. You will need this for paper filings.

What happens next

We’ll add your business to the public list of authorised agents.

You can sign in to your Companies House account to access your authorised agent account: https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk

Here you can:

  • add people who work for the business to the account
  • update the authorised agent details
  • file online as an authorised agent
  • tell us you have verified someone's identity

Your responsibilities as an authorised agent

You, or another member of the authorised agent must:

  • tell us if there is a change to your Anti-Money Laundering (AML) registration status, or the supervisory body details you have provided – you must do this within 14 days
  • provide more information to Companies House if requested – this could be about your business, or records of your filings with Companies House
  • keep records of any evidence used to verify someone’s identity for 7 years, if you verify the identity of clients for Companies House

What we'll show on the public register

On the public list of authorised agents, we’ll show the:

  • authorised agent’s name
  • date the agent registered
  • agent’s registration status
  • town or city of the business address, or the correspondence address if the agent is a sole trader
  • names of all AML supervisory bodies the agent is registered with

When you or another member of the authorised agent verifies someone's identity for Companies House, we'll show the:

  • authorised agent’s name
  • names of all AML supervisory bodies the agent is registered with
