What happens next

We'll email you to let you know if your application to register an authorised agent has been accepted or rejected.

We'll process the application as soon as possible. This is a new service, so we're not able to tell you exactly how long this will take.

If the application is accepted

We'll add your business to the list of Companies House authorised agents.

You'll be able to sign in to your Companies House account to access your authorised agent account. You can:

  • add people who work for the business to the account
  • file online as an authorised agent
  • tell us you have verified someone's identity
  • update the authorised agent details

We'll also send you an authorised agent number. You'll need this to file on paper or using software.

We'll email the authorised agent number to james@morrisaccounting.com. You should keep the number secure and must not share it with anyone outside of your organisation.

If the application is rejected

We'll email you at james@morrisaccounting.com to explain what to do next.
