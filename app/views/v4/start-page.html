Use this service to apply to register as an authorised agent with Companies House.

What you'll need

You must be the relevant officer for the agent to use this service. Read the guidance to check if you are the relevant officer.

Verify your identity

Once you’ve signed in to your Companies House account, you’ll need:

  • to sign in or create a GOV One Login
  • a biometric passport, or a UK photocard driving licence
  • your current home address (you might also need your previous address if you’ve recently moved)

Information about the business

For the business you’re registering, you’ll need:

  • the UK registered address
  • the name of the Anti-Money Laundering (AML) supervisory body the agent is registered with
  • the AML registration number
  • to pay the £XX registration fee using a credit or debit card

Depending on the type of business you are registering, you may need to give more information. Read the guidance to check what information you’ll need.
