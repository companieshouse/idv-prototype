Use this service to register an authorised agent with Companies House.

What you'll need

To register a business as an authorised agent, we'll ask you to verify your own identity first, if you have not done this already. You’ll need to create or sign in to a GOV.UK account to do this.

To register the authorised agent, you'll need:

  • the name of the company, or your own name if you are self-employed
  • the registered address
  • the name of the Anti-Money Laundering (AML) supervisory body the business is registered with
  • the AML registration number
  • to pay the £XX registration fee using a credit or debit card

You’ll also need to give details about the business you are registering. Read the guidance to check what information you’ll need.
